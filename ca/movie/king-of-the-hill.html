The verdict

King of the Hill is a solid, watchable English series that mostly delivers on what it promises. It carries a 7.4/10 on TMDB across 678 ratings, which puts it comfortably above average. Episodes run about 22 minutes. In Canada you can stream it on Disney Plus.

Critics loved it — special praise for the animation and originality. — distilled from critics' published reviews

Story

Set in Texas, this animated series follows the life of propane salesman Hank Hill, who lives with his overly confident substitute Spanish teacher wife Peggy, wannabe comedian son Bobby, and naive niece Luanne. Hank has conservative views about God, family, and country, but his values and ethics are often challenged by the situations he, his family, and his beer-drinking neighbors/buddies find themselves in.
